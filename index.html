About Urban AI

Urban AI focuses on creating sustainable, circular solutions for urban environments by leveraging artificial intelligence and data-driven tools. Our goal is to help cities transition to more efficient resource use, fostering a circular economy.

Our Mission

We aim to integrate AI and digital tools into urban planning and resource management to drive the adoption of circular practices globally. Our projects focus on practical, scalable solutions for cities and communities.

Projects

  • Global Resource Cadastre: Developing a comprehensive database to facilitate circular construction practices.
  • Waste Picker Cadastre: Empowering informal waste pickers through AI-driven mobile tools to track and utilize reusable materials.
  • AI-driven Circular Economy Tools: Advanced tools and frameworks for scaling circular economy efforts.
